About

Hi, I'm Csaba Bai, a papercraft modeler since 2010. Initially my focus was making cars, which helped me to develop my 3D modeling and texturing skills. Since I'm also a spaceflight enthusiast, I designed spacecraft related models as well. All of my models are based on 1:96 scale, however I try to make them as detailed as possible, so rescaling to larger sizes shouldn't be a problem. I personally choose this scale, because this is a good compromise for building rockets and their spacecrafts at the same time.
